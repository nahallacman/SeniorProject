503 * drawn. xL, yT specifies the left top corner and xR, yB specifies the right bottom corner.

515 * yB - y location of the lower right center in the x,y coordinate.
516 * r1 - The smaller radius of the two concentric cicles that defines the thickness
517 * of the object.
518 * r2 - The larger of radius the two concentric circles that defines the thickness

526 * - bit4 : fifth octant
