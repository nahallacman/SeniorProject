183 * - SCROLL_PAGE_6 : page 6

186 * See pageStart for valid values.
187 * frameInterval - sets the frame interval per scroll.
188 * - SCROLL_INTERVAL_6 : 6 frames per scroll
189 * - SCROLL_INTERVAL_32 : 32 frames per scroll
